VRTSync KML Sublayer Color System
Community Category (Landscape Logic)

These should feel natural and vegetation-oriented.

Sublayer	Color	Hex	Reason
bluegrass_area	Turf Green	#2E8B57	Deep grass color, clear on satellite
native_area	Sage Green	#8F9779	Dry/native ecosystem tone
landscape_bed	Mulch Brown	#8B5A2B	Matches mulch/soil visually
pet_station	Teal	#1ABC9C	Utility feature, stands out but not alarming
Irrigation Category (Water Infrastructure)

Use cool mechanical blues so the category feels cohesive.

Sublayer	Color	Hex	Reason
backflow	Bright Cyan	#00BFFF	Water system primary device
master_valve	Deep Blue	#1F4E79	Control infrastructure
flow_meter	Aqua	#00CED1	Water measurement
qc_iso_valve	Light Blue	#87CEEB	Field access points

(Controllers and zones rotating palette will sit nicely next to these)

Snow Category (Winter Operations)

Snow layers should feel operational and visible over snow imagery.

Sublayer	Color	Hex	Reason
plow	Snow Blue	#4A90E2	Standard snow route color
atv	Royal Purple	#6A5ACD	Smaller equipment, distinct
hand_shovel	Magenta	#E83E8C	Human labor areas
ice_melt	Bright Orange	#FF8C00	Material application
slicer	Red	#D62828	Mechanical ice cutting
storage_area	Slate Gray	#708090	Material staging
Trees Category

💡 Pro Tip for VRTSync rendering

For polygons and lines:

fillOpacity: 0.35
strokeOpacity: 0.9
strokeWidth: 2

This keeps them visible on satellite imagery without blocking the map
